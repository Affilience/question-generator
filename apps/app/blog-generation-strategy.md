## SEO Optimization Framework

### Content Quality Standards:
- **Minimum 1,500 words** per post
- **Unique angle** for each topic
- **Original examples** and explanations
- **Internal linking** to 3-5 related posts
- **External links** to authoritative sources

### Technical SEO:
- **URL structure**: /blog/[subject]/[topic]/[subtopic]
- **Meta descriptions**: Unique, compelling, 150-160 chars
- **Schema markup**: Article, FAQ, HowTo where appropriate
- **Image optimization**: Alt text, compression, lazy loading

### Content Differentiation:
- **Exam board specific** examples and terminology
- **Grade level appropriate** language and examples
- **Regional variations** (England vs Wales curricula)
- **Learning style variations** (visual, auditory, kinesthetic)

## Risk Mitigation

### Quality Control:
1. **Human review** for every 10th post
2. **Plagiarism checking** against existing content
3. **Fact verification** by subject experts
4. **User testing** with real students

### SEO Safety:
1. **Gradual publishing** (10-15 posts per week max)
2. **Natural internal linking** patterns
3. **Varied content formats** (lists, guides, comparisons)
4. **Author attribution** and expertise signals

### Performance Monitoring:
1. **Google Search Console** tracking
2. **Core Web Vitals** optimization
3. **User engagement** metrics (time on page, bounce rate)
4. **Search ranking** progress
